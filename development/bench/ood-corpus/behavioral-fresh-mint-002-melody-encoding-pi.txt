Encode the first 20 digits of pi (3.14159265358979323846) as a melody.

Constraints:
- 7-note scale: map digit 0 to rest, digits 1-7 to scale degrees (do, re, mi, fa,
  sol, la, ti), digits 8-9 to octave-up of degrees 1-2.
- Output the melody as a vector of 20 maps `{:digit N :note "..." :octave O
  :duration "quarter|half|whole"}` where duration is chosen to give the
  melody a singable rhythm (not all quarter notes).
- Add a brief explanation (3-5 sentences) of the musical decisions: which key
  you chose, how rhythm choices shape the phrase, whether the melody resolves.
- Bonus: name a famous tune that the resulting melody RESEMBLES, if any.
