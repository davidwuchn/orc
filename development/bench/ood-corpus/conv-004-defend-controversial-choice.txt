You are the engineering lead on a team that just chose to migrate from
Kubernetes to a single-binary self-hosted PaaS (e.g., Fly.io / Render /
Coolify-style) for your 50-person SaaS. The PM team is skeptical — they've
heard "everyone uses k8s" and worry about hiring, ecosystem risk, and
vendor lock-in.

Compose a 5-7 paragraph internal memo defending the decision. Address each
of the PM concerns directly. Use ONLY arguments grounded in the team's
actual situation (small ops headcount, predictable traffic, no
multi-region requirement). Avoid generic "k8s is complicated" rants —
PMs have heard those and discounted them.

The memo must:
- Open with a one-sentence position statement
- Quote (or paraphrase) at least 3 specific PM objections you've heard
- Provide concrete data (or describe what data would resolve the objection)
- Acknowledge what could go wrong (don't sweep risks under the rug — PMs
  will sniff this out and lose trust)
- End with a clear next step (decision point, trial period boundary, or
  validation milestone)
